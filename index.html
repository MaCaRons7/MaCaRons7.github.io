MY PROJECTS

  • Multi-Agent Simulation Game. A god simulation game powered by large language models. Players take on the role of a deity, bestowing miracles to enlighten humanity or unleashing chaos to sow corruption. The fate of human progress rests in your hands! And what kind of humanity will they evolve into…

  • Study with Confucius is an AI-powered educational game that combines role-playing with ancient cultural learning. Here, you’ll take on the role of one of Confucius’ disciples. Through interactions with AI-NPCs, you’ll delve into Confucius’ life and his profound teachings. Explore ancient Chinese classics like The Analects, The Book of Songs, and The Book of Rites by playing through open-ended interactive game levels. Beyond that, you can create your own Analects-inspired stories within the game, fully immersing yourself in the charm of ancient wisdom. Unlike traditional educational games, powered by AI, “Study with Confucius” enables you to achieve many things impossible elsewhere. You can freely converse with AI Confucius, commission carpenters to craft any handicraft, and even have NPCs brew you a bottle of “Flying Heaven Moutai”! Dive into this world brimming with wisdom and creativity, and embark on your unique learning adventure!

  • Lost in Books is a two-player action strategy tower defense game. Players take on the roles of two spirits who have emerged from a book into the real world. While searching for their way home, they unexpectedly become trapped within a book chronicling the Battle of Red Cliffs, forcing them into a fierce battle!
